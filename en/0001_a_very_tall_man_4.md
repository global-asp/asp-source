# A very tall man

##
His hoe was too short.

##
His doorway was too
low.

##
His bed was too short.

##
His bicycle was too
short.

##
This man was too tall!

##
He made a very long
hoe handle.

##
He made very high door
frames.

##
He made a very long
bed.

##
He bought a very high
bicycle.

##
He sat on a very high
chair.
He ate with a very long
fork.

##
He left his house and
lived in a big forest.
He lived for many
years.
