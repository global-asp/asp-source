# The hungry crocodile

##
Once there was a very
hungry crocodile.

##
He searched for food
slowly and quietly.

And then...

##
POW!!!
The crocodile strikes!

##
After that he is no
longer hungry, and he
is happy.

##
Until he gets hungry
again.
