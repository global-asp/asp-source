# I like to read!

##
I like to read.

##
Who can I read to?

##
My sister is asleep.

##
Who can I read to?

##
My mother and
grandmother are busy.

##
Who can I read to?

##
My father and
grandfather are busy.

##
Who can I read to? I can
read to myself!
