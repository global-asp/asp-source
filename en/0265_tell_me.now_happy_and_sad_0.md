# Tell me...now! Happy and sad

##
Why is the baby crying?

##
I think she is hungry.

##
Why is the boy sad?

##
I think he is missing his
father.

##
Why is the girl happy?

##
She has a new dress.

##
Why is the old man
laughing?

##
He read a good joke.

##
Why is the lady smiling?

##
Her children are reading
quietly.

##
What is this lady
feeling?

##
I think she is feeling
shy.
