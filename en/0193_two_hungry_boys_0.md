# Two hungry boys

##
Ben and Thabo are two
hungry boys.

##
Granny is making ten
cupcakes.
She puts them on a
plate.

##
Ben takes one cupcake.
Thabo takes two
cupcakes.

##
Ben takes four
cupcakes.
Thabo takes two
cupcakes.

##
How many cupcakes did
Ben eat?

How many cupcakes did
Thabo eat?

How many cupcakes
are left for granny?
